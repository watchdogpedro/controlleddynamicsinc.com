## 3. TRADITIONAL T-SLOT LIMITATIONS: THE $47B OPPORTUNITY GAP

### The Friction Problem

**Technical Reality:**
T-slot systems rely on friction (μ = 0.3-0.4 for aluminum-on-aluminum). Under vibration:
- Friction coefficient drops to 0.15-0.25 (oxidation, wear)
- Joint strength decreases **60% after 100,000 cycles**
- Requires retightening every 2-4 weeks in vibration environments

**Market Impact:**
This technical limitation **excludes T-slot from 32% of the market** ($2.67B current, $8.4B by 2030 with growth):
- Precision machining centers
- Robotic work cells
